Grades 1-3 Unit

All About Me and My Family

A beginning-year identity unit built around naming, describing, and sharing simple information.

Planning Snapshot

Core unit design

Best fit for students in WIDA Entering and early Emerging.

Language targets

  • I can name family members.
  • I can describe a person with one or two details.
  • I can share simple information about myself.

Grammar and vocabulary

  • pronouns, possessives, simple present
  • family words, feelings, age, likes, colors
  • sentence frames for introductions and descriptions

Suggested sentence frames

  • This is my __.
  • He is __. She is __.
  • I like __.
  • In my family, we __.
