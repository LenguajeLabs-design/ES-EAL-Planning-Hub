Grades 1-5 Unit

Animals and Habitats

A cross-grade science and language unit with strong potential for differentiation by WIDA level.

Planning Snapshot

Core unit design

Works well across grade bands because the content is age-flexible and language can be scaffolded.

Language targets

  • describe animals and habitats
  • compare two habitats or two animals
  • explain how an animal survives

Differentiation by WIDA level

  • Entering: label, match, use short frames
  • Emerging: compare with sentences and visuals
  • Developing: explain cause/effect and support ideas

Suggested performance task

Students create an oral or written comparison of two habitats using visuals, vocabulary support, and WIDA-appropriate sentence frames.
