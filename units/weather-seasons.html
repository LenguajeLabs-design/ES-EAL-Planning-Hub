Grades 1-3 Unit

Weather and Seasons

A descriptive language unit using daily routines, comparison, and weather vocabulary.

Planning Snapshot

Core unit design

Best fit for WIDA Entering to Emerging learners.

Language targets

  • I can describe the weather.
  • I can talk about clothes for different seasons.
  • I can compare two kinds of weather.

Grammar and vocabulary

  • adjectives, demonstratives, present continuous
  • sunny, rainy, hot, cold, windy, cloudy
  • clothing words and season names

Suggested sentence frames

  • Today it is __.
  • In __, I wear __.
  • Summer is __ than winter.
  • I see __ in this season.
