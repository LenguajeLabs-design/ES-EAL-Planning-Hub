WIDA Emerging

Learners growing into more independent academic language.

Quick Guide

What to expect and what to provide

These students can handle more language, but still need support with structure, vocabulary, and text complexity.

Students can often

  • retell with support and describe with several details
  • compare, explain, and sequence using frames
  • write connected sentences and short paragraphs
  • identify who, where, and when, and ask simple clarification questions

Teachers should provide

  • sentence frames for compare/explain/justify tasks
  • supported reading and writing with vocabulary preview
  • partner talk and structured discussion routines
  • shared opinion tasks, simple retell practice, and illustrated text support
