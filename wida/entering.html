WIDA Entering

High-support learners at the beginning stage of English development.

Quick Guide

What to expect and what to provide

These students need strong visual support, repetition, routines, and clear modeling.

Students can often

  • match words to pictures or objects
  • label, point, choose, and complete short frames
  • follow simple repeated classroom routines
  • show understanding with drawings, gestures, acting out, or yes/no signals

Teachers should provide

  • visuals, gestures, realia, and repeated language
  • choice-based responses and oral rehearsal
  • short tasks with one clear language target
  • picture-supported retell, agreement/disagreement signals, and modeled talk
