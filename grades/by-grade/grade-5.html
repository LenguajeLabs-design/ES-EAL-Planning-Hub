Grade 5 Unit Order

This page shows the Grade 5 school-year unit sequence and a simple EAL connection for each unit.

Planning note

  • Keep WIDA supports visible, but raise the cognitive demand through discussion, explanation, and evidence.
  • Use these units to align EAL with homeroom while keeping tasks accessible.

Grade 5

School year unit sequence

Unit 1

Who We Are

Aug 25 to Oct 10

Central idea: Dispositions and skills impact learning.

EAL connection: Teach reflection language, learning behaviors, goals, and strategy talk through short discussion and self-assessment frames.

Unit 2

Where We Are in Place and Time

Oct 13 to Nov 21

Central idea: Migration results from challenges and opportunities.

EAL connection: Build cause/effect and movement language with maps, timelines, and structured speaking about reasons for migration.

Unit 3

How the World Works

Nov 25 to Jan 23

Central idea: The transformation of energy allows us to utilise its power.

EAL connection: Focus on scientific explanation, process language, and key vocabulary with diagrams, sequence frames, and oral rehearsal.

Unit 4

Sharing the Planet

Jan 26 to Mar 20

Central idea: Understanding the Earth's systems helps people develop solutions to maintain a sustainable planet.

EAL connection: Build language for systems, interdependence, impacts, and solutions with compare, explain, and justify tasks.
