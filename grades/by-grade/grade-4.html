Yearly Overview

Grade 4 Unit Order

This page shows the Grade 4 school-year unit sequence and a simple EAL connection for each unit.

Planning note

  • Use these units as the content anchor for upper-elementary language planning.
  • Push students toward compare, explain, persuade, and justify with support.

Grade 4

School year unit sequence

Unit 1

Who We Are

Sept 1 to Oct 17

Central idea: Our inherited traits and cultural experiences interact to shape our identities.

EAL connection: Build language for identity, traits, culture, and belonging with compare-and-contrast frames and personal response writing.

Unit 2

How We Express Ourselves

Oct 20 to Nov 21

Central idea: Different forms of expression can be persuasive.

EAL connection: Focus on persuasive language, audience, opinion, and techniques through supported discussion and short written responses.

Unit 3

Where We Are in Place and Time

Nov 24 to Jan 23

Central idea: Humans evolve through technological innovations and adaptations that shape how people live and work.

EAL connection: Teach time language, change over time, inventions, and evidence-based explanation using timelines and artifacts.

Unit 4

Sharing the Planet

Jan 26 to Mar 20

Central idea: People create positive change in response to environmental issues.

EAL connection: Build vocabulary for issues, solutions, responsibility, and action with problem-solution speaking and writing frames.
