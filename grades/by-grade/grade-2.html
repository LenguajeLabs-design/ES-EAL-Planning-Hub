Yearly Overview

Grade 2 Unit Order

This page shows the Grade 2 school-year unit sequence and a simple EAL connection for each unit.

Planning note

  • Keep language work close to the grade topic.
  • Use drawings, labels, sentence frames, retell, and partner talk to lighten the language demand.

Grade 2

School year unit sequence

Unit 1

Who We Are

Aug 25 to Sep 26

Central idea: There are different ways groups can work together to achieve a goal.

EAL connection: Teach teamwork language, group roles, and sentence frames for agreement, turn-taking, and simple reflection.

Unit 2

Where We Are in Place and Time

Sep 29 to Nov 7

Central idea: Where we live affects how we live.

EAL connection: Focus on map language, landforms, describing places, and compare/contrast frames linked to home and community.

Unit 3

How We Express Ourselves

Nov 10 to Jan 9

Central idea: Understanding and appreciating different cultures promotes international mindedness.

EAL connection: Build vocabulary for traditions, celebrations, and culture through picture-supported discussion, simple retell, and personal connections.

Unit 4

How We Organize Ourselves

Jan 12 to Mar 13

Central idea: Businesses operate within a community by providing goods and services.

EAL connection: Teach community, buying and selling, wants and needs, and functional speaking for asking, offering, and describing services.
