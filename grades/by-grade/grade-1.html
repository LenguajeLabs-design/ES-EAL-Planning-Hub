Yearly Overview

Grade 1 Unit Order

This page shows the Grade 1 school-year unit sequence and a simple EAL connection for each unit.

How to use this page

  • Use the school unit as the content anchor.
  • Match EAL tasks to the same topic, but lower or raise the language demand by WIDA level.

Grade 1

School year unit sequence

Unit 1

How We Organize Ourselves

Aug 22 to Sept 30

Central idea: Systems are created by people with roles and responsibilities.

EAL connection: Teach classroom roles, routines, sequence words, and simple responsibility language such as “I do,” “you do,” and “we help.”

Unit 2

Who We Are

Oct 9 to Nov 14

Central idea: Our choices and actions affect our growth.

EAL connection: Build language for feelings, health, choices, and routines with short sentence frames such as “I can,” “I need,” and “I choose.”

Unit 3

How the World Works

Nov 17 to Jan 16

Central idea: Materials are chosen and transformed for different purposes.

EAL connection: Focus on describing materials, comparing properties, and explaining simple changes using picture support and oral rehearsal.

Unit 4

How We Express Ourselves

Jan 19 to March 13

Central idea: Stories are shared in many ways and express different ideas.

EAL connection: Use retell frames, character vocabulary, and oral storytelling to help students sequence events and talk about story ideas.
