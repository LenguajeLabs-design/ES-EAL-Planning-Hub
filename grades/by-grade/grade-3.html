Yearly Overview

Grade 3 Unit Order

This page shows the Grade 3 school-year unit sequence and a simple EAL connection for each unit.

Planning note

  • Grade 3 is a strong bridge year for retell, explanation, and supported discussion.
  • Use sentence frames, visuals, and structured talk to move students toward longer academic language.

Grade 3

School year unit sequence

Unit 1

Sharing the Planet

Aug 25 to Sep 19

Central idea: Our choices and actions are shaped by different environments.

EAL connection: Build vocabulary for environments, living things, choices, and responsibility with compare-and-explain speaking frames.

Unit 2

How We Organize Ourselves

Sep 19 to Nov 7

Central idea: Communities consist of systems to meet people’s needs.

EAL connection: Use maps, models, and oral explanation to teach community systems, needs, and simple cause/effect language.

Unit 3

How We Express Ourselves

Nov 10 to Jan 16

Central idea: Ideas, feelings, and emotions can be expressed in a variety of art forms.

EAL connection: Focus on opinion language, response stems, feeling vocabulary, and comparing different art forms.

Unit 4

Who We Are

Jan 19 to Mar 13

Central idea: The choices we make impact the health of our body systems.

EAL connection: Build explanation language around health, body systems, habits, and personal choices using diagrams and oral rehearsal.
